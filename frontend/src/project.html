Project Developers

Rainbow City Ugbisi, is under the co-ordination of Rainbow Network Afrique Online Ltd.; a company whose intent and purpose is to develop a network of Rainbow Cities, throughout Africa and beyond.
With its ready team of Engineers, Builders, Consultants, Project Managers and other members of the multi-disciplinary construction team, the 1,000 houses proposed for The Royal City Ugbisi, with associated infrastructures, will be safely built and delivered to the satisfaction of all stake-holders; and this shall be to the glory of God.

Development strategy

The approach of Rainbow Network in developing the Rainbow City Ugbisi and elsewhere is, to promote excellent affordable housing. To this extent, she will with integrity, be the direct supplier of cheaper, high quality, basic construction inputs, beginning from land to sand, from cement to blocks, from wood to roof, from rods to deck, from windows to doors, from paint to painter, until reaching easy and satisfactory completion of each house.

Payment for land by installment

In line with the aims of Royal Network Afrique, to promote affordable housing with ease and convenience, it has put in place, a process of installment payment for lands on sale at the Rainbow City Ugbisi. With this, clients have the option to own property without stress. The payment for land based on this arrangement can be instant, or spread into a span of three months, six months, nine months or even twelve months, as the client may choose…see payment schedules.

Land Purchase (Special Services)

The management of Rainbow City Ugbisi, offers potential buyers living far from the estate, a long distance purchase option, which consists of five elements:
1. You may purchase your land by direct fund transfer to any of the two designated company accounts: ... see account details.

2. At an agreed fee, the company will process all relevant ownership documentations, building designs and design approvals, with all other pre-construction permits. These will be mailed to the purchaser by courier, or if mutually agreed, the documents shall be kept securely, with First Bank of Nigeria Plc.
3. Based on professional Quantity Survey assessments and real estate valuations, we will, by an agreed contract, receive payments from you and build your house in accord with the Architectural and other construction specifications, in the contract, while you may be completely absent.
4. In addition, by an agreed contract, our company may manage your completed property for you as Real Estate Agents, that is, if you live far from Ugbisi in Udu or if you live abroad (in diasporas).
5. As owner of the property, after construction, if you choose to sell it for profit, then by an agreed property sale contract, we may also avail the resources of our company, to sell the property for you and deposit your money in account(s) of your choice.
