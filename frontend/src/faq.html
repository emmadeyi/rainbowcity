A Mini Plot In Rainbow City Is A Piece Of Land Measuring; (60ft. X 50ft.) Or (18mts. X 15mts.). You May Call It Half Plot
On A Mini Plot, you can build a Bungalow having;
One Bedroom, Two Bedroom or Three Bedroom Flat,
or A Storey Building with Two Flats ((up and down),
or A Complete Three Bedroom Duplex
The Land of a Rainbow Mini Plot Sells for N300,000, with a facility fee of N300,000.
The Total Price Is N600,000 Only
Facility Fee is the money you pay, to enjoy the extra benefits of living in a planned estate, such as in Rainbow City.
These includes access to:
24 Hour Internal Security, Fenced Estate,
Tarred Roads, Concrete Drainage, Street Lights,
Regular Power Supply, 24 Hour Petrol Station,
Shopping Mall, Hospital, Schools,
Bank Atms, Church, Gymnasium,
Recreation Centre and Commuter Motor Park.
There are two ways.
1) You may pay instantly ( which means paying at once).
In this case you will enjoy a discount of N50,000; therefore instead of paying N600,000, you will pay, a Total Of N550,000 only.
2) You may pay with ease, by spreading your payment in instalments. There are four instalment plans.
A) 6 month plan, in which you pay N100,000 per month = N600,000, (with No Service Charge).
B) 12 month plan, in which you pay N50,000 per month = N600,000, (with N25,000 Service Charge).
C) 18 Month Plan, in which you pay N33,500 per month = N603,000, (with a Service Charge Of N47,000).
D) 24 Month Plan, in which you pay N25,000 per month = N600,000, (with a Service Charge Of N75,000).
